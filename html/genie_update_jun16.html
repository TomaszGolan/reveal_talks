z-expansion


  • Parametrization of form factors:

\[F_A(q^2) = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{k_{max}} a_k z(q^2)^k\] \[z(q^2, t_{cut}, t_0) = \] \[\frac{\sqrt{t_{cut} - q^2} - \sqrt{t_{cut} - t_0}}{\sqrt{t_{cut} - q^2} + \sqrt{t_{cut} - t_0}}\]

where \(t\), \(a_k\) are arbitrary numbers

A. Meyer et al. arXiv:1603.03048 [hep-ph]
